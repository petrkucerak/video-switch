LCDpackage.vhd - it adds only 2 constants, the sizes of LCD, it is nor necessary

testbench_DisplayLogic.vhd - *** you can run this file only in ModelSim,

VGAgenerator.vhd, VGARegDE2.vhd,  - files used in testbench
